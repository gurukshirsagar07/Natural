** PLACE'S TO VISIT**

VETAL TEKDI

Vetal Tekdi (Vetal Hill) is Pune's highest point, a popular green lung in the city known for sunrise/sunset views, birdwatching (over 240 species!), and fitness activities like hiking, running, and cycling, featuring trails leading to the Vetal Temple, an observation deck, and an old quarry lake, drawing nature lovers and fitness enthusiasts daily, especially from areas like Kothrud and Pashan.

P.L. Deshpande Garden (Pune-Okayama Friendship Garden)

The P.L. Deshpande Garden, also known as the Pune-Okayama Friendship Garden, is a 10-acre Japanese-style garden in Pune, inspired by Japan's famous Koraku-en Garden, symbolizing Indo-Japanese friendship with serene landscapes, koi ponds, waterfalls, and traditional elements like stone lanterns, offering a tranquil escape with walking paths and cultural vibes, plus a separate Mughal Garden section nearby.

Rajiv Gandhi Zoological Park & Wildlife Research Center

The Rajiv Gandhi Zoological Park & Wildlife Research Centre in Katraj, Pune, is a major conservation and education hub, spread over 130 acres, featuring a zoo, a snake park, an animal orphanage (Rescue Centre), and the historic Katraj Lake, focusing on naturalistic habitats, endangered species breeding, and wildlife awareness for visitors, managed by the Pune Municipal Corporation. It's known for its diverse fauna, especially Western Ghat species, offering facilities like battery-operated vehicles and veterinary care, though it's closed on Wednesdays.

Osho Teerth Park

Osho Teerth Park in Pune's Koregaon Park is a serene, 12-acre green oasis transformed from a barren, polluted stream area into a beautiful public park, featuring winding paths, ponds, waterfalls, bamboo groves, and Buddha statues for meditation, offering a peaceful escape with free entry, though cameras aren't allowed and timings are split (mornings & evenings). Maintained by the Osho International Meditation Resort and the Shunyo Foundation, it's a popular spot for strolling, yoga, and connecting with nature, demonstrating ecological restoration.
